What is Nonograph?

Nonograph is a minimalist publishing platform. Anonymous by design: no accounts, no tracking, no compromise. Just write and publish.

Your posts are completely public once published. Anyone with the link can read them, so share the link wisely. We store only the text you write—no IP addresses, cookies, or personal information.

Why Use Nonograph?

  • No accounts: Just write and publish instantly
  • Anonymous: We don't track who you are
  • Open source: Free software you can trust
  • Clean design: Focus on your words, not distractions
  • Rich formatting: Beautiful typography and markup
  • Permanent: Your posts stay online indefinitely

How to Format

Type "/" for commands, or use markdown syntax directly:

  • /h1 or # Heading - Large heading
  • /h2 or ## Heading - Medium heading
  • /bold or **text** - Bold text
  • /italic or *text* - Italic text
  • /link or [text](url) - Links
  • /list or - item - Bullet lists
  • /quote or > text - Block quotes
  • /code or `text` - Inline code

Plus images, videos, tables, footnotes, and more. Add ".md" to any post URL to see the raw markdown.

Publishing

Tap Publish when ready. Posts go live immediately and stay online as long as the servers remain up. 128,000 character limit—enough for long articles.
